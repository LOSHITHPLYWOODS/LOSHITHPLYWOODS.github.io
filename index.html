About Us

Welcome to LOSHITH PLYWOODS & HARDWARE, your one-stop shop for all your woodworking needs. Our company offers a wide range of high-quality products and services, including plywood sheets, door sets, windows, hardware tools, wood moldings, kitchen drawers and fittings, adhesives, and brushes.
With years of experience in the industry, we are dedicated to providing our customers with the best possible products and services. We pride ourselves on offering a wide selection of products to meet the needs of our customers, whether they're professional woodworkers or DIY enthusiasts.
We are committed to providing our customers with a hassle-free shopping experience. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you with your queries and help you find the right products for your needs.
At LOSHITH PLYWOODS & HARDWARE, we believe in building long-lasting relationships with our customers. We are dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and ensuring that our customers are satisfied with their purchases. Our commitment to customer satisfaction is our top priority.
Thank you for considering LOSHITH PLYWOODS & HARDWARE for your woodworking needs. We are confident that our wide range of products and services, combined with our commitment to customer satisfaction, will exceed your expectations.
